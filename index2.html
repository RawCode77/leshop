Our story...

Welcome to the heart of Montreal’s vibrant downtown, where our small shop has been a cornerstone of daily convenience and community spirit. Nestled among the bustling streets, we’ve been serving locals and visitors alike with a smile and a personal touch. From the early morning rush to late-night cravings, our shelves are stocked with a variety of snacks and beverages to satisfy any palate. Whether you’re in need of a quick energy boost or a refreshing drink, we’ve got you covered. We understanding the needs of our clientele, we offer a selection of day-to-day products, catering to those who appreciate quality and convenience. we also have installed a reliable ATM bank machine right in our store, ensuring that you’re never caught without cash when you need it most. And for those feeling lucky, our lotto station is the starting point of dreams. Who knows? The next big winner could be you! Since our doors first opened, we’ve been more than just a shop; we’ve been a friendly face, a helping hand, and a trusted part of your day. We take pride in being here for you, offering the everyday items you need with the exceptional service you deserve. Here’s to many more chapters together in the heart of Montreal.
